Good to know

How the final price is set

EUR · excludes VAT

All prices are estimates in EUR and exclude VAT, confirmed after an inspection of configuration, access and condition.

Condition surcharge

Heavy soiling, oil traces, hardened insects, mould, deep stains or persistent odours: +20–60%, agreed before work starts.

Access & timing

Night work, weekends, urgent deadlines or difficult airport / hangar access: +15–30%.

Wet vs dry wash

Wet wash only where the airport / hangar allows water use and proper wastewater handling — otherwise dry wash is used.

Safety first

No work on sensors, pitot / static ports, antennas, avionics or critical systems without operator / authorised approval.

Aviation-safe products

pH-neutral solutions, ammonia-free glass care, materials compatible with aviation leather, plastics and carpets — SDS available.
