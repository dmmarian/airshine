Safety & Products

Aviation-safe by default.

Manufacturer-compatible

Products compatible with aircraft manufacturer or operator recommendations, with SDS available on request.

Ammonia-free glass

Aviation-specific, ammonia-free window products applied with clean microfiber towels.

pH-neutral dry wash

Paint-compatible dry-wash solutions — never aggressive automotive degreasers.

Cabin-safe materials

Interior products suited to aviation leather, plastics, carpets and flame-retardant materials.

Critical systems protected

No work on sensors, pitot/static ports, antennas, avionics or panels without operator approval.

Tested before polish

Before Level 4, a local test and written confirmation protect paint, coating and composites.

Heavy contamination

Severe dirt, oil, hardened insects, mold or odors: 20–60% surcharge, confirmed before work.

Access & timing

Night, weekend, urgent deadlines or difficult hangar access: 15–30% surcharge.

Airport fees
